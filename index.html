About us

Welcome to Bartex, your trusted partner in logistics and supply chain solutions. With a steadfast commitment to reliability, efficiency, and innovation, we provide tailored logistics services that meet the dynamic needs of businesses across industries. At Bartex, we understand that every shipment is more than just a delivery—it’s a promise. From local distribution to global freight, our expert team ensures that your goods arrive on time, every time. We leverage advanced technology, a vast network of carriers, and years of industry expertise to offer seamless solutions that drive your business forward.

What we offer?

Road shipment

Efficient, reliable, and flexible, our road freight services are tailored to meet your business needs. Whether it’s local or cross-border transportation, we ensure timely delivery with utmost care. Our modern fleet, coupled with real-time tracking, guarantees secure and efficient transportation of goods. From full truckloads (FTL) to less-than-truckloads (LTL), our scalable solutions provide cost-effective options without compromising quality.

Air shipment

When speed matters most, our air freight services deliver. Designed for businesses requiring rapid and reliable shipping, we ensure your cargo reaches its destination without delay. Partnering with leading airlines and leveraging optimized routes, we provide a seamless door-to-door air freight experience. From urgent documents to large shipments, we handle it all with precision and care.

Sea shipment

Cost-efficient and dependable, our sea freight services connect your business to global markets. Whether you need full container load (FCL) or less-than-container load (LCL), we offer flexible solutions for businesses of all sizes. Our extensive carrier network, combined with robust logistics expertise, ensures your cargo is transported securely and efficiently across the seas.
